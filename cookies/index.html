This page explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Departed Digital website.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website remember preferences, improve performance, or measure how the website is used.

How this site may use cookies

  • Essential cookies that support core website functionality.
  • Analytics cookies, if enabled, to understand how visitors use the site and which pages are most helpful.
  • Third-party cookies from embedded tools or hosted services, where applicable.

Managing cookies

You can usually manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the website behave.

Third-party services

If the website uses third-party services such as analytics, fonts, or hosting providers, those services may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy notices.

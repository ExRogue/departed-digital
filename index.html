What We Do

Everything handled.
Nothing for you to worry about.

We take care of the digital paperwork so your family can focus on what matters.

Account Removal

We formally request deletion of Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, LinkedIn, Snapchat and more — following each platform's bereavement process correctly.

Memorialisation

Prefer to preserve their digital presence? We can convert Facebook and Instagram accounts into permanent memorials, giving friends and family a place to remember.

Email & Subscriptions

We handle Gmail and Outlook closures, and unsubscribe your loved one from mailing lists — stopping painful reminder emails from arriving in the family inbox.

Written Confirmation

At every step, we send you written confirmation of actions taken — so you have a clear record, and never have to wonder what's been done.

Process

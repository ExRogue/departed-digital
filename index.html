What we do

What this takes off a family's plate

The point is not more admin. It is a calm, done-for-you route through the accounts that often remain active after a death, with clearer handling and less chasing.

🔒

Account Removal

We submit formal removal requests to Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, and other platforms so you do not have to keep chasing them yourself.

🕊️

Memorialisation

If you want to preserve the profile rather than remove it, we handle memorialisation where the platform allows it.

📋

Full Documentation

We manage the forms, the document requirements, and the correspondence, then keep a clear record of what was submitted and resolved.

💬

Ongoing Support

When platforms are slow or unclear, we follow up and keep the case moving instead of leaving you to do the emotional admin alone.

Founder note

This service is meant to feel more like support than software.

Families do not need another queue, another long form, or another demand for account access. The service is designed to feel handled: short first step, clear package choice, documents later, and a written record at the end.

Departed Digital is built around one idea: the digital admin after a death should feel quietly handled, not dumped back onto the family. Every stage is there to lower friction, reduce uncertainty, and keep the case moving with dignity.

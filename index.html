What we do

One less thing to worry about

Social media removal after bereavement is an exhausting, bureaucratic process. We navigate every platform on your behalf.

🔒

Account Removal

We formally request deletion of your loved one's accounts across Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, LinkedIn, and more — leaving no trace behind.

🕊️

Memorialisation

Prefer to preserve their presence? We convert Facebook and Instagram profiles into memorial pages, keeping memories alive while protecting the account.

📋

Full Documentation

We handle all platform specific forms, death certificate uploads, and correspondence — you simply provide the details, we do everything else.

💬

Ongoing Support

Our team is available to guide you through every step. We chase platforms that are slow to respond and keep you updated throughout the process.

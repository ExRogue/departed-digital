How it works

The partner handoff is intentionally simple.

The model is designed so the family journey stays joined up while your team only needs to make the introduction.

1

Introduce the service

Share the referral card, printed pack insert, or direct link with the family when appropriate.

2

Family starts the case

The family completes the short intake and moves into the private case review step directly with us.

3

We handle fulfilment

We collect the supporting documents after payment, manage submissions, and keep the case moving.

4

Referral closes out

Once the case is completed, the referral fee is processed and the partner record is closed.

Partner FAQ

Common questions from funeral directors.

What does the family pay?

Families choose from the public case packages: Essential at £149, Standard at £249, and Estate at £399. The fee covers the handling and documentation for the selected level of support.

What counts as a converted referral?

A referral counts as converted when the family proceeds with one of the packages and the case is opened for handling.

Do we need to collect documents from the family?

No. We collect the death certificate and proof of authority after payment through our own case flow, so your team is not pulled into document handling.

What do you need from us operationally?

Usually just the introduction. We can provide referral materials so the handoff feels natural inside your existing aftercare process.

Checklist

What to gather before you enquire

You do not need to have everything ready. This list just makes the first conversation faster and clearer.

Who the case is for

The full name of the deceased, your relationship to them, and whether you are acting as a family member, executor, or funeral director referral.

Known platforms

Any accounts you already know about such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Apple, Google, or email providers.

Core documents

A death certificate is usually needed. Some platforms also require proof of relationship or executor authority, which we will confirm before submission.

What happens after your enquiry

This keeps the process honest and manageable: first we scope the case correctly, then we take payment, then we start work.

1

You send the case details

Use the pre-filled email below so we receive the package choice and the basics of the case in one place.

2

We review the case, tell you whether the package is right, and confirm which supporting documents will likely be required.

3

Once you are happy to proceed, we send the secure payment link and open the case for processing.

After payment

Sensitive documents are requested in the next step, not the first one.

Once payment is confirmed, the case moves into the supporting-document stage. That is where we request the death certificate and proof of authority needed for the platforms involved.

Typical follow-up documents
  • Death certificate
  • Proof of relationship or executor authority
  • Any platform-specific evidence needed for the case
